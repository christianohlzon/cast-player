CastPlayer

Drop video here or click to select.

Cast local videos with subtitles to your Chromecast.

Uses the Chrome browser's native Cast controls.

Supports HTML5 video formats (MP4, WebM, OGG) and some MKV files.
Subtitles must be in SRT or WebVTT format.

To display subtitles when casting DON'T press "Optimize" when you start casting.
